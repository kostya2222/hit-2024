Dear Colleagues,
We invite you to take part at the Ninth International Conference on Humic Innovative Technologies "Humic substances and green technologies” (HIT – 2024), which is held under auspices of the Regional CIS Chapter of the International Humic Substances Society (IHSS) and the Lomonosov Moscow State University, Department of Chemistry.
Conference dates:
October 25-28, 2024
Conference Venue:
Sailing Club "Vodnik", Dolgoprudny, Moscow Region, Russia
The goal of the conference is to bring to awareness of broad scientific community the latest achievements in the research on both life-sustaining functions of humic substances and their reproduction in nature-like materials, processes, and technologies. These studies are of particular relevance in the context of green technologies (GreenTech). The key areas of GreenTech include waste recycling, minimization of environmental impact, conservation of natural resources, protection of climate and human health, development of new agricultural technologies, and designing nature-like products and technologies. These areas are in compliance with modern trends in practical uses of humic substances. Another important and necessary focus of the conference is development of theoretical principles of the humic systems chemistry including chemical definition, standardization, and humification theory.
We invite all IHSS members and conference participants to look at humic substances and technologies in the context of GreenTech!
The important part of the conference is the Fifth exhibition of humic products and technologies. All business participants are cordially invited to take part in the Exhibition!
The conference will take place in a hybrid format: face-to-face and online participation. We will be very glad to see you this year in the offline and online spaces within the framework of the conference.
Welcome to HIT-2024!
